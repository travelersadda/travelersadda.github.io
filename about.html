Who We Are

We are storytellers, explorers, and budget-hackers on a mission to show you the world.

It Started With a One-Way Ticket

Back in 2018, three friends sat in a cramped coffee shop with a map, a tiny budget, and a massive dream. We quit our corporate jobs, packed 40-liter backpacks, and bought one-way tickets to Bangkok.

Travelers Adda was born out of our mistakes. We got lost, missed trains, and fell for tourist traps. We created this platform so you don't have to. Today, we are a global community of wanderers sharing honest, vibrant, and practical travel guides.

"Travel isn't about escaping reality. It's about letting reality escape you."
